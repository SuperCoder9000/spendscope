# SpendScope Product Metrics

## Core Product Metrics

### Activation

* Users generating an audit
* Users completing lead capture

### Engagement

* Audit completion rate
* Time spent on audit page
* Shareable report usage

### Retention

* Returning users
* Repeat audits
* Email re-engagement

### Revenue Metrics

* Potential monthly savings identified
* Qualified enterprise leads
* Infrastructure credit referrals

## Success Indicators

Short-term:

* strong audit completion rate
* high shareability
* positive user feedback

Long-term:

* recurring spend monitoring adoption
* enterprise onboarding
* procurement optimization partnerships
